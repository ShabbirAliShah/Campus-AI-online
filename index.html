When you add a Groq, Gemini, or OpenRouter API key, your messages are sent directly from your browser to that provider. We do not proxy or store your messages. Review each provider's privacy policy before use.

Privacy Policy
Data We Collect
Nothing. Campus AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. There is no user account system.
What Stays in Your Browser
The following is stored in your browser's localStorage only and never leaves your device: your chat history, API keys, generation settings, and bookmarks. Clearing your browser data removes all of this.
Third-Party API Providers
When you send a message using a cloud provider (Groq, Gemini, OpenRouter), your message is sent directly from your browser to that provider's API. Their respective privacy policies apply. We do not act as an intermediary or store your messages.
Image Generation
Image prompts sent to Pollinations.ai or Hugging Face are subject to their respective privacy policies.
Cookies & Analytics
We use localStorage (not cookies) for preferences. If Google Analytics is enabled on this deployment, anonymized usage data may be collected per Google's privacy policy. No personally identifiable information is sent.

About Campus AI
What is Campus AI?
Campus AI is a free, browser-based AI assistant built for students and educators worldwide. It requires no account or installation — just open the page and start chatting.
Welcome to Leisure Campus
Founded in 2019 by Syed Shabbir Jan in Chitral, Pakistan, Leisure Campus is a digital educational platform built to bridge the technology divide for students in remote regions. We build tools that work with or without reliable internet.
Our Mission
To empower students in underserved communities with free, accessible technology — ensuring that geography or connectivity should never limit access to quality education.
Our Products
Campus AI: Free web-based AI assistant powered by Groq, Gemini, and OpenRouter.
Leisure Campus App: Connects students with teachers and mentors for coaching and academic guidance.
Khowar Digital Dictionary: Linguistic resource preserving the Khowar language with audio pronunciations.
Local Internet (Mesh Wi-Fi): Distributes educational resources across communities without a global uplink.
